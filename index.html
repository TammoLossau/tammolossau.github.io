I am a postoctoral reasearcher ("wissenschaftlicher Mitarbeiter") at the Institute of Philosophy at the University of Bremen. There, I am part of the working group of Norman Sieroka. My main research interests are standards, particularly epistemic standards. Aside from that, I have worked on knowledge ascriptions, ancient skepticism in Western and Eastern philosophy and the work of John Mackie. Prior to working in Bremen I was a Visiting Assistant Professor at Ashoka University, and prior to that I completed my PhD at Johns Hopkins University.
